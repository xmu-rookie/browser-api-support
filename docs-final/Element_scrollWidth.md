## 详细兼容性信息

### Chrome

- **支持版本**: 1

### Edge

- **支持版本**: 12

### Firefox

- **支持版本**: 1

### Internet Explorer

- **支持版本**: 5
- **注意事项**:
  - In Internet Explorer 5 through 7, if padding is set, the value of `scrollWidth` is equal to the sum of the left and right padding. This behavior was fixed in Internet Explorer 8.

### Opera

- **支持版本**: ≤12.1

### Opera Android

- **支持版本**: ≤12.1

### Safari

- **支持版本**: 1
